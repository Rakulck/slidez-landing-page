# Slidez – Product Marketing Context

## What is Slidez?
Slidez is an AI stylist and virtual try-on app. Users describe what they want to wear (e.g. "date night look", "business casual but edgy") and the AI generates full outfit recommendations. Users can also virtually try on clothes before buying.

Available as:
- iOS & Android app (primary) — download link: https://linkly.link/2FWYm
- Chrome extension — https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/kdcmgmfnnheiegkakcbkdolehlgdlaak

## Core Value Props
1. AI outfit generation — describe a vibe, get a complete outfit
2. Virtual try-on — see how clothes look on you before buying
3. Fast — "style yourself in seconds"
4. Free to use

## Target Audience
- Primary: Women aged 18–35 who shop online
- Secondary: Fashion-conscious shoppers who want to try before they buy
- Pain points: decision fatigue when getting dressed, buying clothes that don't work together, returns from poor fit/style choices
